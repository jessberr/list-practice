Applesauce-Banana Bread

This bread is a blue-ribbon winner.

  • 1/3 cup shortening or oil
  • 1/2 cup honey or 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • Applesauce
  • 1 medium banana, mashed
  • 1/4 cup sour milk or buttermilk
  • 2 cups whole flour
  • 1/3 teaspoon soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)
  • 1/2 cup raisins chocolate chips

Cream shortening, honey or sugar, and eggs. Add enough applesauce to mashed banana to measure 1 cup. Combine with the creamed mixture. Add milk, dry ingredients, nuts, and chips. Mix until just moistened. Pour into lightly greased loaf pans.

Bake: 350 ℉ for 40-50 min

Yield: One 4" x 8 1/2" or Two 3" x 5 3/4" loaves

Calories per Serving:
93
